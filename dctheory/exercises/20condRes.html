If the length of a copper conductor is L feet and is A circular mils in area:

What is the total resistance of this conductor in ohms?

(K*L)/A

If the length of a awg gauge copper conductor is L feet:

What is the total resistance of this conductor in ohms?

(K*L)/awgCM

The answer for CM sized wire has to be within .005 ohms, and the answer for AWG sized wire has to be within .01 ohms

The K factor for copper is 10.4 ohms/cm/ft according to the textbook.
